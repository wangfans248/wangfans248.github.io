About

I am an undergraduate student in Robotics Engineering at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), expected to graduate in 2028.

I am interested in how intelligent systems acquire structured understanding of the world—moving beyond surface-level pattern recognition toward models that reason, adapt, and interact in a principled way. Currently, I am exploring structural anomaly detection, controllable image generation, and the application of reinforcement learning in diffusion models.

I am actively seeking research opportunities for Summer 2026. Feel free to reach out!
